Know Your Passenger Rights
For grievances related to the Civil Aviation Requirement (CAR), Section 3 – Air Transport, Series M, Part IV, please refer to the redressal process outlined below.

The Government of India is committed to ensuring that your flying experience is smooth, safe, and fair. The Passenger Charter outlines your rights and responsibilities while travelling by air with any Indian airline or through airports in India.
